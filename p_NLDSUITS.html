Next Level Devils
& SUITS
The Next Level Devils are a collaborative team of students who strive to develop solutions both as innovators and professionals while raising awareness of current challenges in the aeronautics and astronautics field.

We focus on NASA's annual Micro-g NExT, SUITS, and RASC-AL challenges. As these challenges involve developing thoroughly-conceived solutions to genuine problems NASA faces, they require an interdisciplinary effort.

NASA SUITS (Spacesuit User Interface Technologies for Students) challenges students to design and create user interfaces for various assets on the lunar surface. Here is a link to the NASA SUITS website: NASA SUITS.

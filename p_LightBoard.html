LED Light Board
I created a detailed video showcasing how I built a custom LED light board using an ESP32 and an LED light strip. In the video, I walk through the entire process, from the initial setup of the hardware to the coding and wiring involved in making dynamic sprites move across the board. It's a fun project where I demonstrate how the ESP32 can control the LED strip, allowing me to create smooth animations and bring the sprites to life.

Space Jump
SpaceJump is a fun and exciting platformer game created by yours truely using Unity. Inspired by the classic Doodle Jump, the game features a space man who must jump from platform to platform, navigating through an endless, retro-themed space environment. Developed in less than a week, SpaceJump offers a casual and cute experience with a sci-fi twist. While there may be a few minor bugs and glitches due to the quick development time, the game still delivers an enjoyable and addictive experience. Available for Windows, SpaceJump is perfect for anyone who enjoys simple yet engaging indie games with a nostalgic retro vibe.

Install Instructions:

1.) Download and extract the .zip file here.

2.) Go to SpaceJump>PC Build 4>SpaceJump.exe.

3.) In Windows Defender, click "more info" and "run anyways" (Don't worry it is not a virus).
